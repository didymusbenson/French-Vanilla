# Privacy Policy for French Vanilla

**Last Updated: January 19, 2026**

## Overview

French Vanilla is a Magic: The Gathering comprehensive rules reference app designed to help players browse, search, and bookmark MTG rules.

## Data Collection and Usage

**We do not collect, store, transmit, or use any personal data whatsoever.**

- **No data collection**: The app does not collect any information about you or your device.
- **No analytics**: We do not use any analytics services to track app usage.
- **No third-party services**: The app does not integrate with any third-party services that would collect data.
- **Local storage only**: All app data (bookmarks, search history, settings) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and never leaves your device.
- **No internet connection required**: The app functions entirely offline with all rules data embedded in the app. No server communication occurs.
- **No advertising**: The app contains no advertisements or advertising frameworks.

## In-App Purchases

French Vanilla offers optional in-app purchases (supporter tiers). These purchases are processed entirely by Apple's App Store or Google Play Store:

- **No payment information collected**: We never see or store your payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple or Google.
- **Purchase verification**: The app verifies purchases with the App Store/Play Store to confirm ownership. This communication is between your device and Apple/Google servers only.
- **Purchase state storage**: Your purchase status is cached locally on your device for offline access. This data never leaves your device.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information
- Email addresses or contact information
- Usage data or analytics
- Location data
- Device information
- Crash reports or diagnostics
- Payment or financial information
- Any other personal or non-personal information

## Data Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This includes:
- Bookmarked rules and glossary terms
- Search history
- App settings and preferences
- Purchase state cache

This data remains on your device and is never synchronized to cloud services or external servers.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any data from any users, the app is safe for use by all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices in the future, we will update this privacy policy and the "Last Updated" date above.

Since we do not collect any data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. All app data is under your direct control on your device and can be removed by uninstalling the app.
